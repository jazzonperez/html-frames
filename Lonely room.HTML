In a lonely room of a small island archipelago.

I write to you to impart my portrayual, my life as I know it.

I implore you, however, not to let yourself be dissuaded by such nuances. Reasons can only impede what you wish to impart, if not restrict you to adhere to the ideas and perceptions of the other people who may wish to discourage you from doing such an endeavor.


by: Jazzon Perez
